So what is IoT ?
IoT or "Internet of Things" is about connecting devices over the internet,
letting them talk to us and each other. So by doing this we can control
our machines or our appliances from anywhere around the globe.
By making them smart we can automate the process and
hence increase efficiency and bring down production cost.
IoT is the future. It is the next big thing.
